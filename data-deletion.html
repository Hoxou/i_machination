Data Deletion Instructions

If you want to delete your data from our service, please follow the instructions below:

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Send an email to ihoxen@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include your name, username, and the email address associated with your account.
  3. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm via email once your data has been deleted.

If you have any questions about this process, please contact us at ihoxen@gmail.com.
